Computes a discount curve by interpolation of logs of market discount factors. The construction of the discount curve is accomplished in 3 steps:

  1. Compute the logarithms of the discount factors rDF.
  2. Interpolate the logarithms of the discount factors with rInterp.
  3. Recover the discount curve from its logarithmic interpolation.
Parameters
rTimeThe maturities of known discount factors.
rDFThe known discount factors.
dInitialTimeThe initial time.
rInterpThe interpolation method for yields.
Returns
cfl::Function The interpolated discount curve.

Computes a discount curve by interpolation of market yields. The construction of the discount curve is accomplished in 3 steps:

  1. Compute market yields from the discount factors rDF.
  2. Obtain market yield curve by interplation with rInterp.
  3. Compute the discount curve from the yield curve.
Parameters
rTimeThe maturities of known discount factors.
rDFThe known discount factors.
dInitialTimeThe initial time.
rInterpThe interpolation method for yields.
Returns
cfl::Function The interpolated discount curve.
